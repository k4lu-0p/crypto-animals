the cryptoanimals

CryptoAnimals is a 10 000 NFT Collection launched on the Ethereum blockchain.

Hand drawn and automatically generated, the collection pays tribute to meme and pixel art culture. Each crypto-animal gives you membership access into Anima's interoperable 2D world and to the Keepers community.

discover anima

Anima is a FREE and persistant MMORPG that uses blockchain to provide a new social and gaming experience to the committed actors and communities of the metaverse. We want to provide a new way for socially and culturally committed projects to create utility and to establish colonies in the Anima World.

We want to support cultural projects and animal welfare projects by giving them visibility. Our mission is to gather people sharing the same values and ambitions in order to create together a more eco-responsible, social and cultural metaverse that will allow to carry out actions for the good. A system of colony will allow to integrate others NFT communities in the game and create custom places for their colonies. Your place, your rules.

Because we want to have an impact in the "physical" world. A pourcentage of the funds generated by the sale of the game collections will be placed in a wallet and dedicated only to charitable actions or partner non-profit organizations.

join the keepers

By owning a CryptoAnimals, you will become a Keeper. Being a Keeper allow you to have access to increasing benefits over time and in game exclusive features in the Animaverse.

In addition Keepers will be able to hangout with their CryptoAnimals as avatar and in the form of "Companion" in the Anima.

Keepers will have an impact on the game and governance rights in a custom DAO system. We want the game to be community driven and decentralized.
